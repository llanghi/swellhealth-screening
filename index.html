Screening tools

Short, structured screening questionnaires to help patients reflect on symptoms before discussing them with a GP or health professional.

Before you start

These tools are provided for screening and information only. They do not provide a diagnosis and should not replace clinical assessment or advice from a qualified health professional.

If you feel unsafe, at immediate risk, or are worried you may harm yourself or someone else, seek urgent help now by calling emergency services or attending the nearest emergency department.
